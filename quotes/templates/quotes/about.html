About

Wayne Douglas Gretzky CC is a Canadian former professional ice hockey player and former head coach. He played 20 seasons in the National Hockey League for four teams from 1979 to 1999.

He is the leading scorer in NHL history, with more goals and assists than any other player. He is the only NHL player to total over 200 points in a single season, a feat he accomplished four times. He also holds or shares 61 NHL records, including most career goals (894), most career assists (1,963), and most career points (2,857).
